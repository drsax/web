DrHong

10 Solo Albums, 1 Saxophone book, Jazz / Blues

Saxophonist Dr. Hong studied jazz saxophone at Delta Community Music School in Canada
and got a Doctor of Musical Art In Computer Music (D.M.A) at Dongguk University,
Master of Computer Engineering (M.C.E) at Yonsei University,
Master of Music In Computer Music (M.M) at Dongguk University,
He was also invited as a Visiting Scholar to Plymouth University in UK and
had a speech about "Music Technology" at Carnegie Mellon University.
Various experiences through jam sessions with jazz clubs in Canada and
visiting scholars in the UK and became the motive for creating songs.
He has already released various albums and is trying to express his own unique color with
Jazz, funky, pop, contemporary music based on blues
